How to Report Your Sighting

  1. When spoting a cryptid or any unusual activity first wright down the exact time, date, location.
  2. If you are able to take a photograph or illustration of the cryptid for records
  3. When in a safe location wright down a description/retelling of the events leading upto and after the spotting
  4. Last bring all of the above to a cryptid investigator
